About Us

Minstrel David Royal School of Music is a purpose-driven, forward-thinking institution dedicated to shaping exceptional musicians who can compete and perform on global stages. Built on a foundation of disciplined training, creative exploration, and professional mentorship, the Academy provides a transformative learning experience for students at every level.

Our mandate is to discover, develop, and refine musical talent while empowering individuals to excel in performance, technique, and artistic expression. We combine classical tradition with modern performance techniques, ensuring every student becomes a well-rounded musician. Whether you are a beginner exploring your first instrument or an advanced performer preparing for competitions, we provide the structure, tools, and inspiration you need to thrive.

The Academy integrates practical instruction, music theory, stage performance, and personal development. With modern teaching approaches, well-equipped practice studios, highly-skilled instructors, and personalized coaching for all ages and skill levels, students are positioned to grow into confident, creative, and versatile artists.

Our core values of Spirituality, Discipline, Creativity, Excellence, Integrity, Passion, and Continuous Growth guide our culture and reflect our unwavering commitment to raising outstanding musicians ready to perform on both classical and contemporary stages.

How to Apply

Follow these three simple steps to begin your journey with Minstrel David Royal School of Music.

1

Step 1: Submit Your Details

Fill out a short pre-registration form with your name, phone number, email, and preferred program.

2

Step 2: We Contact You

One of our Enrollment Officers will contact you via phone, WhatsApp, or email to confirm your details, answer any questions you may have, and explain class options, schedules, and fees.

3

Step 3: Complete Registration & Payment

After confirmation, you can complete your registration in the way most convenient for you. Visit the school to finalize your registration and payment, or pay securely online through an official payment link sent to you.
