Who We Are

Shaping World-Class Musicians

Minstrel David Royal School of Music is a purpose-driven institution dedicated to shaping exceptional musicians who can compete and perform on global stages. Built on disciplined training, creative exploration, and professional mentorship.

Our mandate is to discover, develop, and refine musical talent while empowering individuals to excel in performance, technique, and artistic expression. We combine classical tradition with modern performance techniques, ensuring every student becomes a well-rounded musician.

The Academy integrates practical instruction, music theory, stage performance, and personal development. With modern teaching approaches, well-equipped practice studios, and personalized coaching for all ages and skill levels, students grow into confident, creative artists ready for global stages.

Our core values of Spirituality, Discipline, Creativity, Excellence, Integrity, Passion, and Continuous Growth guide everything we do.

Piano & Keyboard

Vocal Training

Voice development, range extension, and worship-led vocal technique for every level.

How to Apply

Three simple steps to begin your musical journey with Minstrel David Royal School of Music.

1

Submit Your Details

Fill out a short pre-registration form with your name, phone number, email, and preferred program.

2

We Contact You

An Enrollment Officer will reach you via phone, WhatsApp, or email to confirm your details, answer questions, and explain class options and fees.

3

Register & Begin

Complete registration in person or pay securely online through an official payment link sent directly to you.
